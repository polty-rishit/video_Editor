About Me

I am passionate about technology, and have very good problem-solving, troubleshooting, and creative thinking skills. I have been video editing for over 3 years, learning from my father, who creates videos professionally. I am comfortable in Final Cut Pro, and familiar with Premier Pro. I have edited videos for multiple companies, other's projects, social media, internal corporate videos, and home videos.

Have learned about various aspects of technology, encountering and troubleshooting hundreds of issues. I also work with my local teen tech center, assisting them with their technological needs, such as grant allocation, setup, maintenance, and upgrading their laptops, and consulting on many of their decisions. I also volunteer at electronics recycling centers.

In my spare time, I enjoy working on my own tech projects, such as fixing old laptops, recovering data, building computers, pushing the limits of software, 3D modeling, graphic design, tech consulting, and collecting and cataloging old tech. I also enjoy riding my bike, seeing the city, sitting around a bonfire, hanging out with people, and analyze media.
